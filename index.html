Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2025

Overview

Minmax Sleep Tracker is a personal health data visualization tool that connects to your WHOOP account to display sleep and recovery metrics.

Data Collection

The App accesses sleep data, recovery scores, and strain data from your WHOOP account.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your personal device. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal health data with any third parties.

Security

OAuth tokens are stored locally. Only you have access to your health data.

Your Rights

You can revoke access at any time through your WHOOP account settings.

Contact

For questions, contact the app administrator.
